About Us

We take community projects to the next level by building a community, hosting conferences, and offering mentorship. Get involved today!

Are you a young person ready to change the world between the ages of 12 - 22? Get involved with our projects or let us guide you in starting your own!

Are you an adult, business, or other organization looking to make an impact in GenZ? We would love to collaborate with you. Become a mentor, sponsor or donor today!

Our Mission

We strive to bring change-makers together and empower teens to change their community and the world. Through mentorships, networking, and youth development we are helping take teen projects to the next level. We strive to help teens certify their organizations and create an association of changemakers! We also take in students looking for ways to get involved in helping the world.
